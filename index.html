2D Commissions

Choose from sketches, line art, flat colors, or shading. Prices vary by character coverage (portrait, upper half, or full body), additional characters, and background complexity.

  • Sketch:
    • Portrait: $8
    • Upper Half: $10
    • Full Body: $12
  • Line Art:
    • Portrait: $12
    • Upper Half: $14
    • Full Body: $16
  • Flat Colors:
    • Portrait: $16
    • Upper Half: $18
    • Full Body: $20
  • Shading:

Whether you need low-poly models reminiscent of the PS1 era or high-detail PS3-quality models, I offer fully rigged and textured creations. Additional options include viseme setup and dynamic props.

    • Facial Viseme Setup: +$10
  • From-Scratch Props:
    • Static Prop: $30

  • Kitbashing:
