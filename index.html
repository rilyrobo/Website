Commissions and Pricing

Interested in personalized artwork? Explore my 2D and 3D commission options below:

2D Commissions

Choose from sketches, line art, flat colors, or shading. Prices vary by character coverage (portrait, upper half, or full body), additional characters, and background complexity.

  • Sketch:
    • Portrait: $50
    • Upper Half: $75
    • Full Body: $100
  • Line Art:
    • Portrait: $60
    • Upper Half: $90
    • Full Body: $120
  • Flat Colors:
    • Portrait: $70
    • Upper Half: $110
    • Full Body: $150
  • Shading:
    • Portrait: $80
    • Upper Half: $130
    • Full Body: $180

Additional Costs:

  • Additional Characters: +50% per character
  • Backgrounds: $50–$150 depending on complexity
  • NSFW Content: +20% base cost

3D Commissions

Whether you need low-poly models reminiscent of the PS1 era or high-detail PS3-quality models, I offer fully rigged and textured creations. Additional options include viseme setup and dynamic props.

  • From-Scratch Models:
    • Low-Poly (PS1 style): $300
    • High-Poly (PS3 style): $500
    • Facial Viseme Setup: +$150
  • From-Scratch Props:
    • Static Prop: $100
    • Dynamic Prop (with rigging): $200
  • Kitbashing:
    • Character Kitbash: $250
    • Prop Kitbash: $150

Additional Costs:

  • NSFW Content: +20% base cost
